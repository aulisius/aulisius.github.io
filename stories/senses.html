you don't need all your senses to love

Written on May 6th, 2020

when our eyes meet, when the sight of you makes my day better,
when our eyes tussle and try to pierce each other's soul

I realise
you don't need all your senses to love

when our fingers touch,
when your palm fits perfectly in mine,
when you hold me

I realise
you don't need all your senses to love

when I hear your footsteps,
when I hear the sound of your laughter, your anger and your silence

I realise
you don't need all your senses to love

when the air starts to smell like you,
it makes me feel safe,
makes me feel loved,
makes me realise

you don't need all your senses to love

in every bite that I take,
in every kiss that we share

I realise
you don't need all your senses to love
